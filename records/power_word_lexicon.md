# The New Man's Power-Word Lexicon (SKJV)

This glossary compiles the Top 30 Operational Words used in the Simple King James Version (SKJV) translation. By linking these terms to their original Greek and Hebrew root definitions, this lexicon serves as a practical field guide to the mechanics of the New Covenant.

---

### 1. COMMAND (Eipē - G2036)
* **Definition**: An executive order, decree, or command of authority.
* **Mechanism**: Used by Jesus in Mark 11:23. It means the believer does not beg the obstacle or pray to God about it; the believer addresses the obstacle directly with a commanding decree.

### 2. HEART (Kardia - G2588)
* **Definition**: The seat of thoughts, desires, identity, and core convictions; the spiritual engine room.
* **Mechanism**: To doubt within the heart is to run a divided script. The heart must be single-minded and aligned with the mouth for faith-commands to carry execution power.

### 3. BELIEVE / TRUST (Pisteuō - G4100)
* **Definition**: To credit, entrust, commit to, or have absolute confidence in.
* **Mechanism**: Believing is not an intellectual agreement; it is an active state of reliance and expectation that manifests in physical declarations.

### 4. MOUTH (Stoma - G4750)
* **Definition**: The physical organ of speech used to formulate and issue decrees.
* **Mechanism**: The mouth is the "printer" of the heart. What is settled in the heart must be spoken through the mouth to establish reality in the physical realm.

### 5. DELEGATED AUTHORITY (Exousia - G1849)
* **Definition**: Legal right, official jurisdiction, and executive command backed by a higher government.
* **Mechanism**: Christ handed the believer His "badge" (Luke 10:19). The believer does not fight with raw muscle, but commands with the legal standing of the Kingdom of God.

### 6. SUPERNATURAL POWER (Dunamis - G1411)
* **Definition**: Inherent, explosive, miraculous force, and dynamic ability.
* **Mechanism**: The "dynamite" of the Holy Spirit (Acts 1:8). When the believer speaks with delegated authority (*exousia*), the Holy Spirit releases supernatural power (*dunamis*) to execute the command.

### 7. SICKNESS / DISEASE (Astheneia - G769 / Nosos - G3554)
* **Definition**: Bodily weakness, physical frailty, or structural failure of the body.
* **Mechanism**: Translated in traditional Bibles as "infirmities." The SKJV uses "sicknesses and diseases" to expose these conditions as physical intruders that have been legally paid for and evicted.

### 8. WOUNDS / SCOURGING WOUNDS (Molops - G3468 / Chabburah - H2250)
* **Definition**: Physical injuries, welts, or bloody gashes caused by scourging.
* **Mechanism**: Traditional Bibles use "stripes." The SKJV translates this as "wounds" to keep the physical reality of the whipping post clear. Jesus's wounds were the currency paid for physical bodily health.

### 9. SINS (Hamartia - G266 / Paraptoma - G3900)
* **Definition**: Missing the mark, lawlessness, or violations of spiritual covenant.
* **Mechanism**: Traditional Bibles use "trespasses." The SKJV uses "sins" to simplify the legal vocabulary for immediate modern understanding.

### 10. A GRUDGE (Lupe - G3076 / Bitter Offense)
* **Definition**: Bitterness, active sorrow, or offense held against another in the heart.
* **Mechanism**: Holding a grudge forms a double-minded loop in the heart (Mark 11:25). It acts as a "power eraser" for the tongue, which is why forgiveness must be immediate.

### 11. REPENTANCE (Metanoia - G3341)
* **Definition**: A total change of mind, direction, and structural thinking.
* **Mechanism**: Repentance is leaving behind the old man's logic and adopting the New Covenant operating system. It is laid once as a foundation (Hebrews 6:1).

### 12. DEAD WORKS (Nekros Ergon - G3498 / G2041)
* **Definition**: Religious box-checking, legalism, and fleshly efforts to earn what God has already given.
* **Mechanism**: Believers are commanded to leave behind the loop of repentance from dead works and press on to mature power execution.

### 13. GOOD WORKS (Agathos Ergon - G18 / G2041)
* **Definition**: The pre-ordained lifestyle, actions, and power demonstrations of the New Man.
* **Mechanism**: We are created in Christ to walk in good works as our default daily lifestyle.

### 14. NEW MAN (Kainos Anthropos - G2537 / G444)
* **Definition**: The brand-new, re-created spiritual identity of the believer in Christ.
* **Mechanism**: The New Man does not manage sin; the New Man manages Kingdom power, operating in perfect righteousness and authority.

### 15. SEPARATING TONGUES (Diamerizo Glossa - G1266 / G1100)
* **Definition**: Dividing or distributing languages of fire.
* **Mechanism**: Replaces the confusing KJV term "cloven tongues" (Acts 2:3) to accurately depict the distribution of the Spirit's flame to each individual believer.

### 16. CONVICTION (Katanysso - G2660)
* **Definition**: To pierce or cut deeply into the heart.
* **Mechanism**: The action of the Holy Spirit exposing a double-minded life or sin to lead an individual to repentance.

### 17. DOMINION (Radah - H7287 / Katakyrieuo - G2634)
* **Definition**: Reigning, governing, and exercising administrative control.
* **Mechanism**: The original commission of mankind restored under the New Covenant to exercise authority over the earth and demonic elements.

### 18. HEAL (Therapeuo - G2323 / Iaomai - G2390)
* **Definition**: To cure, restore to health, and enforce physical soundness.
* **Mechanism**: Healing is not a petition; it is an action of authority executed over the body.

### 19. CAST OUT (Ekballo - G1544)
* **Definition**: To drive out, expel, or evict by force.
* **Mechanism**: Command-level execution over demonic forces, treating them as illegal trespassers.

### 20. FAITH (Pistis - G4102)
* **Definition**: Persuasion, trust, and absolute reliance on God's covenant.
* **Mechanism**: Faith is a present-tense law that commands results in the physical world.

### 21. CONFESSION (Homologia - G3671)
* **Definition**: Saying the exact same thing; speaking in agreement with God's law.
* **Mechanism**: To confess is to align the mouth's printout with the Covenant contract.

### 22. OBSTACLE / MOUNTAIN (Oros - G3735)
* **Definition**: A mountain or high ground; figuratively, any physical or situational barrier.
* **Mechanism**: In Mark 11:23, the mountain represents any obstacle (sickness, lack, blockages) that must bow to the spoken decree.

### 23. LISTEN CLOSELY / LOOK (Idou - G2400)
* **Definition**: Pay attention! Mark my words!
* **Mechanism**: Modern attention anchors replacing the archaic "behold" or "lo."

### 24. STAND TO PRAY (Steko Proseuchomai - G4739 / G4336)
* **Definition**: Active, commanding posture in prayer.
* **Mechanism**: Replaces passive kneeling or begging; standing represents readiness to declare and enforce covenant law.

### 25. FORGIVE (Aphiemi - G863)
* **Definition**: To send away, let go, or cancel a debt.
* **Mechanism**: The active release of all grudges to keep the channel of faith unblocked.

### 26. MANIFEST (Phaneroo - G5319)
* **Definition**: To make visible, clear, and physically real.
* **Mechanism**: The process where spiritual realities become physical facts.

### 27. COVENANT (Diatheke - G1242)
* **Definition**: A legally binding contract between two parties.
* **Mechanism**: The New Covenant is a finished legal agreement signed in the blood of Christ.

### 28. RIGHTEOUSNESS (Dikaiosyne - G1343)
* **Definition**: Right standing; the legal status of being in perfect alignment with Kingdom law.
* **Mechanism**: Righteousness is a gift that grants the believer bold access to the throne of authority.

### 29. MASTERPIECE (Poiema - G4161)
* **Definition**: That which is made; handiwork or artistic creation.
* **Mechanism**: Replaces the KJV "workmanship" in Ephesians 2:10 to highlight the beauty and authority of the New Man.

### 30. MATURITY (Teleiotes - G5047)
* **Definition**: Perfection, completion, or full growth.
* **Mechanism**: The destination of the believer who leaves behind elementary repentance to build the house of faith.

### 31. HOLY SPIRIT / GHOST (Pneuma Hagion - G4151 / G40)
* **Definition**: The literal spiritual Person of God who dwells within the believer.
* **Mechanism**: The SKJV uses "Holy Spirit" for peak modern readability, replacing the archaic "Holy Ghost." However, the operational reality remains identical: He is a literal, tangible spiritual Person (literally a "Ghost" occupying the believer's body as His temple) rather than an abstract influence or feeling. He works in perfect alignment with the *human spirit* to enforce New Covenant authority on earth.
