# Giga King James Version (GIGAKJV) Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 28, 2026

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This policy details how we treat user information on the Giga King James Version (GIGAKJV) reader website and repository.

---

## 1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal identifying information (PII) when you browse this website or use the offline reader. 
- The Bible reader runs entirely client-side in your web browser.
- We do not use database-backed tracking cookies, analytic tracking pixels, or third-party advertising scripts.
- We do not run any remote tracking telemetry or capture any IP addresses or search queries.

---

## 2. Local Storage

This website uses client-side browser storage (such as `localStorage`) to save your preferences and customize your reading experience. This includes:
- Your selected theme (e.g., light, dark, lora, cinzel, jetbrains).
- Selected font size and view mode settings.
- Your last read book and chapter.

This data remains stored locally on your device, is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties, and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings or by clearing browser site data.

---

## 3. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as [supernaturalchurches.org](https://supernaturalchurches.org)). We do not control, and are not responsible for, the privacy practices, content, or cookie policies of external domains.

---

## 4. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

If you have any questions or feedback regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at: [contact@supernaturalchurches.org](mailto:contact@supernaturalchurches.org).
